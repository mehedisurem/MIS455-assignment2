FRAPPUCCINO

Frappuccino is a line of blended iced coffee drinks sold by Starbucks. It consists of coffee or crème base, blended with ice and ingredients such as flavored syrups and usually topped with whipped cream and or spices.

Price: $5.55

CAPPUCCINO

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Austria with later development taking place in Italy, and is prepared with steamed milk foam. Variations of the drink involve the use of cream instead of milk.

Price: $6.69

LATTE MACCHIATO

First, in a latte macchiato, espresso is added to milk, rather than milk to espresso as in a caffè latte. Second, a latte macchiato features more foam rather than simply hot milk.

Price: $6.21

AMERICANO

Caffè Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from, traditionally brewed coffee.

Price: $4.27

TURKISH COFFEE

Turkish coffee is a style of coffee prepared in a cezve using very finely ground coffee beans without filtering.

Price: $5

CAFE CUBANO

Café Cubano is a type of espresso that originated in Cuba. Specifically, it refers to an espresso shot which is sweetened. However, the name is sometimes used to refer to coffee-based drinks.

Price: $6.99
